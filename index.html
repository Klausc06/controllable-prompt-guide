中文 Prompt
生成一段 9:16 竖屏，约 10 秒，适合短视频平台 的视频。
核心意图：健身房开业宣传视频，突出新店空间、专业器械、活力训练氛围和到店体验感
主体：一家线下门店作为核心主体，门头、室内空间、服务细节和顾客体验清晰可见。
场景：明亮整洁的商业室内空间，布局有序，背景干净，品牌环境清楚。
动作与叙事：用三段式微叙事呈现：先建立场景，再出现动作变化，最后给出清晰结果。
镜头调度：中景构图，主体占据画面主要区域，比例平衡自然；镜头缓慢向主体推进，逐渐加强视觉重点和情绪。
光线：干净棚拍光线，背景简洁，主体受光均匀，商业产品展示质感。
视觉风格：电影级写实风格，画面真实、光影克制、商业广告质感。
声音：搭配轻快积极的背景音乐，节奏适合短视频传播。
画面文字：如需文字，只保留一行短标题，位置清楚、字数少、不要复杂排版。重要文字保持短、清楚、可读。
限制：不使用明星脸、影视 IP、未授权角色或明显品牌侵权元素；主体身份、服装和外观保持一致，不变脸、不畸变、不突然切换主体；如需画面文字，只使用短句，位置清楚，避免乱码、错字和过多字幕。
English Prompt
Generate a 9:16 vertical, around 10 seconds, suitable for short-form social platforms video.
Core intent: gym opening promotional video highlighting the new space, professional equipment, energetic training mood, and visit appeal.
Subject: a local storefront as the main subject, with visible signage, interior space, service details, and customer experience.
Scene: a bright and tidy commercial interior with orderly layout, clean background, and clear brand environment.
Action and story: present a three-beat micro narrative: establish the scene, show the action change, then end with a clear result.
Camera staging: a medium shot with the subject occupying the main area in balanced proportion; the camera slowly pushes in toward the subject, gradually intensifying focus and emotion.
Lighting: clean studio lighting with a simple background, even subject illumination, and commercial product presentation.
Visual style: cinematic realism with controlled lighting and a premium commercial look.
Audio: use upbeat positive background music with pacing suitable for short social videos.
On-screen text: if text is needed, keep only one short title line, clearly placed with minimal typography. Keep important text short, clear, and readable.
Constraints: avoid celebrity likenesses, copyrighted characters, unauthorized roles, or obvious brand-infringing elements; keep the subject identity, clothing, and appearance consistent with no morphing, distortion, or sudden subject changes; if on-screen text is needed, keep it short and clearly placed, avoiding garbled letters, typos, and excessive captions.
